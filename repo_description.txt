DMac: A Multi-Agent AI Swarm

A modular, locally hosted AI ecosystem that coordinates a swarm of specialized agents to perform a wide range of tasks including software engineering, manufacturing automation, and creative design.

Key Features:
- Prioritizes local LLMs through Ollama with strategic use of external services
- Enhanced learning system that improves from all model interactions
- Specialized agents for coding, manufacturing, design, UI, and IoT
- Integrated workflow management and visualization tools
- Continuous improvement through reinforcement learning

Built with Python, Ollama, and a focus on local AI sovereignty.
